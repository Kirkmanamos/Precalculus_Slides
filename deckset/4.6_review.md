##[fit] Graphing Sine & Cosine

---

# Learning Targets

- Graph transformed sine and cosine functions.
- Identify amplitude, period, phase shift, and vertical shift.
- Write equations from graphs.
- Diagnose and correct transformation errors.

# Board Prompt 6

- Amplitude: 1
- Period: $$4\pi$$

# Board Prompt 7

## Error Analysis

A student claims this graph represents:

$$
y = 2\sin(2x - \pi)
$$

But:

- The period appears to be $$2\pi$$.
- The midline is incorrect.

Identify:

1. The mistake.
2. The correct graph features.
3. The corrected equation if necessary.

---

# Board Prompt 8

## Error Analysis

A student graphed:

$$
y = -\cos\left(\frac{x}{2}\right) + 3
$$

But they:

- Reflected incorrectly.
- Applied period after shifting.
- Placed midline at 0.

Explain every mistake and fix it.
